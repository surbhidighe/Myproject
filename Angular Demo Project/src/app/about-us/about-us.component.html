About Us

Carma Media provides our clients with a full range of digital media and marketing solutions. We also specialize in producing original content for television and film. We have worked with over 400 clients ranging from technology, consumer product manufacturers, insurance and healthcare providers to food and beverage, health and beauty products, music artists and non profit organizations. We are also a proud media production partner with the Asian Culture and Media Alliance (ACMA), a 501(c)(3) non profit organization whose mission is to unite, promote and empower the Asian Pacific American community through Media Arts.
